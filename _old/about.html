Who are we?

The idea of RadialApps was conceived in one of the smaller cities of India in 2011, as a multi-purpose firm with focus on software and security. At that time, it started with a budget of zero cents, and clibmed up to come where it is today. We believed that a lot of hard work can lead to success without having unlimited resources. It proved to be true.

What do we do?

Our goal is clear: make something useful and, if possible, something new. We understand that it might not always be easy to achieve this goal, but we still keep trying!

We have ported the entire Aveyond series and the indie game Exit Fate to Linux and Mac. Some of our other exploits include porting Exit Fate and Last Scenario to Android.
